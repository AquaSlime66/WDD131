Overview

Purpose

One of my favorite shows is Breaking Bad, and something I like to do for CSE class projects is make something that relates to me or my interests. Los Pollos Hermanos is a well-known resturant in the world of Breaking Bad, so it would make sense for them to have some system of online ordering, an online menu or even just a website. The resturant is actually a front for some more illict/illegal activities, and that's where the javascript will come in (I will not be doing anything illegal...).

Audience

The likely audience for this is fans of the TV shows Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul. I've enjoyed these shows over the past couple years with several family members and friends, so they are another target audience. The resturant owner, Gustavo Fring, also references a website with a complaint form, so that's an additional page I will add.

Dynamic elements

The home page will be a resturant menu, with all resturant items added dynamically through javascript. A secret menu with references to miscellaneous scenes and such in Breaking Bad appears in place of the menu items when a secret password is used in some location (still figuring that out). I might also add some sort of cart, where the user can add items from the menu to their cart, and see them once they click on it. If that still isn't enough Javascript, the complaint form will definitely use some to hide user details or important information.
